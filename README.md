# Clubs Plus
![enter image description here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/SrilalS/MAD_Project/main/Docs/img/main.jpg)
#### Managing and engaging with dozens of clubs and societies in a university is may be overwhelming. We decided to develop a mobile application for Clubs and Societies in our university So the students may actively engage with these clubs and also giving the change for clubs and societies to engage with students directly.
